Welcome to RVCE

Established in 1963, RV College of Engineering offers 15 UG, 14 PG, and Doctoral programs. Located on Mysore Road, Bangalore, the 16.85-acre campus fosters a serene learning environment. RVCE is ranked among India’s top engineering colleges.
